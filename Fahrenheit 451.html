Author: Ray Bradbury

Originally Published: October 19, 1953

Genres: Novel, Science Fiction, Political fiction, Dystopian Fiction

About it: Guy Montag is a fireman. In his world, where television rules and literature is on the brink of extinction, firemen start fires rather than put them out. His job is to destroy the most illegal of commodities, the printed book, along with the houses in which they are hidden.
